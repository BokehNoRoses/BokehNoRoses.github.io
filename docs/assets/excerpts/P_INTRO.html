I am United States Army Reservist with a background as a Logistics Management Specialist, specifically for providing Chemical, Biological, Radiological, and Nuclear (CBRN) defense capabilities. I have a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science with a concentration in Data Science, in which I was a Junior Data Scientist with a Microsoft Gold Partner for a short stint after university. My handle is based on a python package called bokeh that I used for fun doing geographical data visualations. In the 10 years I've started using computers for programming and IT work, I've developed a fervent passion for the industry as an intersection between arts and science, starting with my first forays into programming using freecodecamp.org and creating my step-father's website using PHP to setting up A/V for teleconferences at the enterprise level. I aspire to make a career out of being a systems administrator that can truly leverage the experiences I had during military service as well as personal pursuits to become a benefit and credit to a worthwhile organization or cause.

My hobbies include:

  • Music Production:

    I have played musical instruments from a young age, starting with recorder to viola to trumpet and even drums and guitar, but I had a long hiatus during my teenage years. In Senior year of university, I picked up my first Digital Audio Workstation (DAW), FL Studio, and I haven't looked back since. I currently use Bitwig Studios to compose flavors of Drum n' Bass, Orchestral, and Electronic music, but I listen to most genres of music. One day, I hope to make an EP I am really proud of.

I cannot wrap my head around many of the higher level techniques, but it's extremely satisfying to perform mental gymnastics while under duress to submit an opponent. I have since done MMA, Jiu Jitsu, and Muay Thai (in that order).
